# Notes
- Completing the square: $(a^2 + bx \pm c) \to (a^2 + bx \pm (\frac{b}{2})^2 = \mp c)$ can be helpful for factoring

# Convert Standard to Vertex Form
1. Isolate $ax^2+bx$
2. Factor out $a$
3. Complete the square and add the term (multiplied by $a$) across the sign
4. Factor
5. Isolate $y$

## Example
$$
  y=2x^2+8x+4
$$

1. $y - 4 = 2x^2 + 8x$
2. $y - 4 = 2(x^2 + 4x)$
3. $y - 4 + 8 = 2(x^2 + 4x + 4)$
4. $y + 4 = 2(x + 2)^2$
5. $y = 2(x+2)^2 - 4$
